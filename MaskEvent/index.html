Murder Masquerade

The Tale of a Murder at Mardi Gras

Febuary 24th | 7:00pm - 11:00pm | Hart House

About The Event

Once Upon a Murder is a night filled with mystery, celebration, and suspense! Be apart of an interactive live game of a murder mystery, set in 1899 New Orleans. The night will be filled with endless entertainment, music, food & drinks, interactive performers, prizes, games, and a little bit of Mardi Gras History, so there’s a little something for everyone!

Story

Set in 1899, New Orleans, at a time when rival Krewes (secret societies) took to the streets for Mardi Gras, the very first female Krewe was formed. The Calypso Krewe has secretly financed the closing Mardi Gras Masquerade Ball, and they have invited the oldest known male society the Sinis Krewe to attend. The masquerade has commenced, but shortly into the night of inhibition, celebration, and intoxication, a Sinis krewe member turns up DEAD. Who at this ball, is the Masked MURDERER!

Murder Mystery

As an attendee, you will be a key player in solving the Mardi Gras Murder Mystery. Engage with our live actors, other guests, and decor in order to solve the mystery. Correct accusations will be entered into a pool for the grand prize, and winners will be drawn live

Entertainment

At the Mardi Gras’ murder mystery you will interact with 8 suspects to crack the case to get a shot at winning the grand prize. While you’re working on solving the mystery enjoy a wealth of our live Champagne Girl, make a quick stop at our Mugshot Photo Booth, interact with our wandering magician for some sleight of hand, enter the tent of the New Orlean’s Voodoo Queen and have your tarot cards drawn if you dare and witness the magic array of spinning lights during our live LED twirling performance!

Food & Beverage

There will be ample opportunity to indulge in an assortment of gourmet h’orderves, and our cash bar.

Win Big

Bid on our amazing silent auction prizes, or play our murder mystery for the chance to walk away with some amazing prizes.

Charity

We are in affiliation with the Covenant House, and all the proceeds from Once Upon a Murder will go to the Covenant House Charity Foundation, and George Brown College Scholarships and Bursaries.
